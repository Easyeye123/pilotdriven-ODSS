# Natural Earth land basemap

`natural_earth_110m_land.geojson.gz.b64` is a gzip-compressed, Base64-encoded copy of Natural Earth's 1:110m land GeoJSON.

- Source: https://github.com/nvkelso/natural-earth-vector/blob/master/geojson/ne_110m_land.geojson
- Downloaded: 2026-07-22
- Source SHA-256: `9e0729ee253ca7d7a5c4ae9395fb1902264c5377c52e224d13dd85010e2835d9`
- Natural Earth terms: https://www.naturalearthdata.com/about/terms-of-use/

Natural Earth data is public domain. ODSS uses this small-scale land layer only for briefing orientation; it is not an aeronautical chart or a navigation source.
