Actual-time operational clock

A timing reference is required for absolute UTC calculations. Enter either the actual takeoff time (ATOT) or an actual time over a route waypoint (ATA). The dashboard derives the time-zero anchor and calculates UTC crossing, early-call and critical-event times while retaining the original ACTM.

Waypoint ATA mode re-anchors the whole route: derived ATOT = entered waypoint ATA − that waypoint's CFP ACTM.
